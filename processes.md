# Standard Operating Procedures

## Onboarding New Customer
1. Welcome email (within 1 hour)
2. Discovery call (within 24 hours)  
3. Contract/SOW signed
4. Project kickoff
5. First deliverable
6. Feedback iteration
7. Final delivery
8. Follow-up for review

## Support Process
1. Customer submits request
2. Acknowledge within 4 hours
3. Investigate issue
4. Provide solution
5. Follow up to confirm resolution

## Sales Process
1. Lead capture (form/email)
2. Initial response (within 4 hours)
3. Discovery call
4. Proposal sent
5. Follow-up (3 days)
6. Close or nurture

## Blog Post Process
1. Topic research
2. Outline creation
3. First draft
4. Humanize/Edit
5. Publish
6. Promote
